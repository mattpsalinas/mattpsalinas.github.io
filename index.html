Current Research

Induced Seismicity Analysis

Completed Honors Thesis with Prof. Yihe Huang at the University of Michigan, analyzing spatiotemporal changes in geothermal induced seismicity sequences. This work involved evaluation of seismic hazard with the b-value to explore potential realtime monitoring strategies. Planning to continue and expand this research at USC.

Rock Deformation

Worked with Prof. Heather Savage at UC Santa Cruz, operating a Rapid Heating Apparatus to measure clay deformation at fault scale times and temperatures. This research included field work in Eastern California and resulted in a presentation at AGU Fall Meeting 2023.
